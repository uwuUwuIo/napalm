# Napalm Archive

Welcome to the Napalm repository! This is an archive dedicated to collecting games, lost media, and other related content.

## Description
The Napalm archive serves as a resource for enthusiasts and researchers interested in exploring lost games, media that has been forgotten over time, and all related materials. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive and accessible collection for future generations.

## Guidelines for Contributions
We welcome contributions from the community! Here are some guidelines to follow:

1. **Quality Content**: Please ensure that your contributions are relevant to the archive and valuable to the community.
2. **Sources**: Always provide sources or references for the media you submit.
3. **Respectful Communication**: Be respectful and constructive in your discussions and contributions.
4. **License Compliance**: Make sure to comply with copyright and licensing laws when submitting any media.

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Napalm archive! Together, we can keep these pieces of history alive.
